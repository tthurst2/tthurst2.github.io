RetroGAN

Github Link RetroGAN Paper

RetroGAN is cycleGAN based GAN network that is trained to automatically convert between 8-bit and 16-bit pixel graphic screenshots.

This was built in ~1 month Unity for GA Tech's CS7643. It consists of a custom built training dataset, custom loss function, and full training/testing results presented in paper.

Overall results were promising(especially for the time), with convincing translations between NES/SNES graphics. SNES -> NES was particularly good, as down-scaling to a more constrained color palette allowed easier training and more convincing fakes.
